Initial Consultation

Our first step is to schedule a meeting to discuss the objectives and scope of your project. During this meeting, we will review design plans, features, and the overall vision. You will also provide any relevant media, logos, or branding materials, or we can assist in creating them as needed.

Design Phase

Once the design concepts are outlined, I will create wireframe mockups of the website to provide a clear visual representation of the final product. After receiving your feedback and confirming the design direction, we will proceed with the development phase.

Development

Once the design is approved, I'll begin coding the website, transforming the finalized plans into a fully functional site. The website will be fully responsive, adapting seamlessly to devices such as mobile phones, tablets, laptops, and desktops

Testing & Launch

Before the website goes live, it will undergo thorough testing to identify and resolve any errors or bugs. Once testing is complete and everything is functioning as expected, the website will be launched via hosting on a secure server, marking the completion of the project.

Pricing Guidelines

The cost of the website will vary depending on the scope and specific requirements of the project. For example, a simple single-page website may take approximately one week to complete and could cost a few hundred pounds. In contrast, a more complex, multi-page e-commerce site may require two months or more of development, with a potential cost of a few thousand pounds.

The final cost will be determined based on the complexity of the work, the time required, and any additional services such as media content creation, branding, large-scale databases, or backend systems. Additionaly hosting and maintenance costs also need to be considered.

An initial rough estimate can be provided, with final pricing confirmed during the consultation. Typically, 50% of the project fee is paid upfront, with the remaining balance due upon project completion.
